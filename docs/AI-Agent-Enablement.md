# AI Agent Enablement

## Introduction

The purpose of Experience Distillation is not only to preserve knowledge.

It is also to make organizational knowledge usable by AI systems.

As AI becomes increasingly integrated into daily work, organizations need structured knowledge that AI can understand, retrieve, and apply.

AI Agent Enablement is the process of transforming organizational knowledge assets into AI-enabled capabilities.

---

## Why AI Needs Experience Distillation

Most AI systems have access to general knowledge.

However, organizations require AI systems that understand:

* Internal practices
* Expert decision patterns
* Organizational standards
* Local workflows
* Context-specific knowledge

Without structured organizational knowledge, AI systems remain generic.

Experience Distillation provides the foundation for organizational AI.

* SOPs
* Playbooks
* Lessons Learned
* Knowledge Cards

### Prompt Assets

* Interview Prompts
* Reflection Prompts
* Facilitation Prompts
* Decision-Making Prompts

### Workflow Assets

* Problem-Solving Workflows
* Knowledge Capture Workflows
* Learning Workflows
* Coaching Workflows

---

## AI Skills

An AI Skill represents a reusable capability.

Examples include:

* Root Cause Analysis
* Experience Extraction
* Workshop Design
* Knowledge Structuring
* Action Planning

AI Skills can be combined to create more advanced agents.

---

## AI Agents

An AI Agent combines:

* Knowledge
* Prompts
* Workflows
* Tools

to perform meaningful organizational tasks.

Examples include:

* Experience Distillation Agent
* Workshop Design Agent
* Knowledge Management Agent
* Learning Design Agent

---

## Vision

The future organization will not only have experts.

It will also have AI agents built upon the experience of those experts.

Experience Distillation provides the bridge between human expertise and organizational AI.

# What is Experience Distillation?

## Definition

Experience Distillation is the process of transforming individual experience into reusable organizational knowledge assets.

It enables organizations to capture, structure, and transfer valuable expertise that would otherwise remain hidden within individuals.

The goal is not simply to document experience, but to make it reusable, teachable, scalable, and increasingly AI-enabled.

---

## Why It Matters

Every organization contains valuable experience.

However, most experience remains:

* Tacit
* Personal
* Context-dependent
* Difficult to transfer

As a result:

* Experts become bottlenecks.
* Knowledge leaves when people leave.
* New employees learn slowly.
* Organizations repeatedly solve the same problems.

Experience Distillation addresses these challenges.

---

## From Knowledge to Capability

Traditional knowledge management focuses on storing information.

Experience Distillation focuses on building capability.

The objective is to answer:

* How do experts think?
* How do experts make decisions?
* How do experts solve problems?
* What patterns lead to success?

---

## Key Principles

### Experience is an Asset

Experience should be treated as a strategic organizational asset.

### Knowledge Must Be Structured

Unstructured experience cannot be effectively transferred.

### Capability Can Be Modeled

Expert performance can be analyzed and represented through models.

### Learning Must Be Reusable

Knowledge should be transformed into assets that can be repeatedly used.

### AI Requires High-Quality Knowledge

AI systems are only as effective as the knowledge they can access.

---

## Outputs

Experience Distillation may produce:

* Knowledge Cards
* Playbooks
* SOPs
* Training Materials
* Competency Models
* Knowledge Graphs
* AI Skills
* AI Agents

---

## Relationship with AI

Experience Distillation provides the foundation for AI-enabled organizational learning.

Without structured knowledge, AI systems cannot reliably support decision-making, learning, or capability development.

Experience Distillation transforms human expertise into forms that AI can understand and apply.

---

## Vision

The future of organizational learning is not simply knowledge management.

It is the continuous transformation of experience into organizational intelligence.
